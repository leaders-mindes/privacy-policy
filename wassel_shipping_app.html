Privacy Policy for Wassel Express - Shipping App

Effective Date: 2025-05-14

Thank you for using Wassel Express - Shipping App. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our Shopify app, which connects your online store with delivery companies using the Wassel Express platform.

1. Information We Collect

To facilitate delivery operations, our app collects the following information from your store:

  • Order details (e.g., customer name, shipping address, contact number, item details)
  • Store information (e.g., store name, store location)
  • Expected Cash on Delivery (COD) value for each order

We do not collect or process any payment information such as credit card numbers or bank details.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Share order and delivery details with the selected courier company
  • Improve the efficiency and accuracy of order fulfillment

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or resell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We apply industry-standard measures to safeguard your data. Order information, including the expected COD value, is only stored temporarily as needed to complete the integration and is not retained unnecessarily.

4. Third-Party Access

Order and delivery information is shared only with courier companies that operate through the Wassel Express platform, strictly for shipment and delivery purposes.

5. Your Rights

You may uninstall the app at any time, which halts all data collection. For data deletion requests or support, contact us at info@leadersminds.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
